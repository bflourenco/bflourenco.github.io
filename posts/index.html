My latest complaints

As a kid, people used to tell me that I complain too much. It is fitting that I became a researcher and can now complain to my heart’s content in a professional capacity. Here I gather my latest complaints.

Opportunities to complain abound in research. Papers often start with complaint about previous papers (a.k.a., the state-of-art review).

Before we decided to bless the world with our wisdom on this topic, a lot was written on the same subject by our lesser colleagues. Their papers all suck and, buckle up kids, we are going to tell you why in excruciating detail…
